How to play
Welcome to Guess The Random Number - 2!
This is an improved version of the first one named "Random Number Guessing Game".
But this time, there are different modes. They are:

Normal Mode: You have to guess a number between 1 to 100 and there will be hints to help you figure out the randomly generated number.

Hard Mode: You have to guess a number between 1 to 100 but you have to guess the number in atleast 10 guesses. If you exceed 10 guesses, you lose the game. There will be no hints given other than if the guessed number is higher or lower than the random number.

Custom Mode: In this mode, you can select a number between 1 and 100 and you can ask your friend to guess the number. You can give hints to your friend to help them guess the number. It is like a multiplayer mode in some way.

Also, when you win, you can screenshot your win and share it with your friends wen you click the share button after you win.
Those are the modes and their rules. Hope you enjoy my game! :)
